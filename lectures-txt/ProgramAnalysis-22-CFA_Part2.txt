===== slide 9 =====
π-calculus: syntax details
• The notion of free and bound names (constants and variables) are defined
as usual: we focus on fc(P) and fv(P)
• The notion of capture avoiding substitution is defined as usual: P[y/x]
• Processes are taken up-to alpha-renaming of bound names:
• u(y) . P = u(z) . (P[z/y]) if z ∉ fv(P)
• (νn) . P = (νn′) . (P[n′/n]) if n′ ∉ fc(P)

• We focus on closed processes, i.e., process P such that fc(P) = fv(P) = ∅
