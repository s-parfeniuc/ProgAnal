Testing

Observe just a nite (small) number of executions

For each execution, check whether it violates the spec,
if no bugs were found, then the program passes the test
Unsound: can accept programs violating the spec
Complete: does not reject programs that satisfy the spec

Not automatic: key proof arguments provided by
users and checked by the system

Quasi-complete: (only limited by the

Perform exhaustive exploration of program states
Automatic, sound & complete (only w.r.t. the nite models)
