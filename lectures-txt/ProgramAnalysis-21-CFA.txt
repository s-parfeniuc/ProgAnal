Adding contexts
• 0-CFA is a context-insensitive (or mono-variant) analysis
• It does not distinguish various instances of program variables and program
points from each other, in particular does not distinguish di erent calling
contexts
• Context-sensitive (or poly-variant) analysis does distinguish
• Examples of such analyses include k-CFA analyses, uniform k-CFA
analyses, polynomial k-CFA analyses and the Cartesian Product
Algorithm
• We focus on uniform k-CFA (“uniform” because environment and cache
have the same precision)
